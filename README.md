# NFLCombineData
An analysis of NFL Combine data from 2000 - 2018. 
Utilizing data from players who participated in the NFL Combine from 2000 - 2018 to try and predict if a player will get drafted based on their 
Combine stats. Additionally, analyzing feature importances and coefficients can provide intereting insights into what impacts the likelihood of being drafted.

Potential future analysis:
- Predicting draft Round and/or Pick
- Predicting draft Team

Data Source: https://www.kaggle.com/savvastj/nfl-combine-data
